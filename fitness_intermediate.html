Gentle Reminder!

Intro to

The Intermediate Exercise

Welcome to the Intermediate Exercise section! This section will start building more muscle but less strength. Our goal is to complete 50 push-ups and 20 pull-ups. We will also learn some cool skills on our way. In order to start, please select a level.








Before We Start

Time Spent

This section will cost you 6-10 month to complete it. During workout, you need do the workout for sets and reps. We will recommend you to do the whole workout for 3 sets each and rest for 3 minutes between each workout.

Recovery Time

Arrangement of workout time is very important for building strength and muscle as the muscle needs enough rest to recover from heavy exercise. We recommend you to do 5 workout sessions per week, but not in a row. Do treat your muscle well!

Diet Control

If you want to build muscle and burn calories, diet control is one of key to achieve your goal. Otherwise, all of your workout will be wasted since you are just increasing the calories in your body that is burned during the workout. Learn more about diet control here!








Your Journey















Intermediate Journey #1

Requirment: Beginner Journey

Working Part Exercises
Push

Regular Push Up

This Journey will start to be harder, check your form strictly! Finish 20 reps to proceed to next journey.
Pull

Negative Pull Up

Find a high bar and a chair that you can step on it. Hold the bar and jump until your chin is above the bar, then slowly come down. Finish 8 reps to proceed to the next journey.
Legs

Bulgarian Split Squad

Place a leg on a surface and another leg on ground, then start doing squad. Don't go too deep! Finish 15 reps each leg to proceed to the next journey.

Core

Basic Crunch

Basic Plank is not challenging anymore! Start doing crunch and do it slowly to get best result. Finish 20 reps to proceed to the next journey.
Full Body

Burpee With Push Up

We will start doing some cardio that work on build muscle and burn fat! Finish 5 reps to proceed to the next journey.











Intermediate Journey #2

Requirment: Regular Push Up x 20, Negative Pull Up x 8, Bulgarian Split Squad x 15, Crunch x 20

Working Part Exercises
Push

Regular Push Ups

Your chest will be burning ðŸ”¥ and pumped up after this! Finish 30 reps to proceed to the next journey
Pull

Band Pull Up

We will recommend band pull up if you have a resistance band, feel free to adjust the width of the resistance band. But if you don't have it just do negative pull up. Finish 12 reps to proceed to the next journey.
Shoulder

120 Degree Pike Push Up

We will start to building up shoulder strength to achieve handstand push up. Are you excited for that? Finish 15 reps to proceed to next journey
Legs

Bulgarian Split Squat with weight

Same exercise but you might need to add some weight, you can hold a heavy stuff on your hand or carry a bag. Finish 15 reps with 5 kg to proceed to next journey
Core

Russian Twist

Sit on the ground, lift up your legs and start twisting your body. Remember to stay stable and touch your hand on each side of the ground. Finish 25 reps to proceed to the next journey.

Full Body

Burpee

Same exercise but you can try doing bulgarian split squad before you jump up! Finish 10 reps to proceed to the next journey.










Intermediate Journey #3

Requirment: Regular Push Up x 30, Band Pull Up x 12, 120 Degree Pike Push Up x 10, Bulagrian Split Squad x 20, Russian Twist x 20, Burpee x 12

Working Part Exercises
Push

Diamond Push Ups

If you are able to do 30 strict push ups in a row, then you should be able to do some Diamond Push Ups. If you can't do it, start doing some of them on your knees. Finish 12 reps of Diamond Push Ups to proceed to the next journey.

Pull

Pull Up

Finally we will be doing Regular Pull Up! Form is the most important part in this exercise, so make sure to follow it strictly. Finish 7 reps to proceed to the next journey.

Shoulder

90 Degree Pike Push Up

I know it is extremely hard but keep pushing! You can spread out your leg if it is too hard. Finish 12 reps to proceed to next journey
Legs

Jump Squat

Time to add some explosive power, jump as high as possible! Finish 25 reps to proceed to the next journey.

Core

Russian Twist

Same as the last one. Finish 50 reps to proceed to the next journey.

Full Body

Burpee

Regular Burpee but a lot more challenging. Finish 30 reps in a row to proceed to the next journey.











Intermediate Journey #4

Requirement: Diamond Push Up x 15, Pull Up x 7, 90 Degree Pike Push Up x 12, Jump Squad x 25, Russian Twist x 50, Burpee x 30

Working Part Exercises
Push

Diamond Push Ups

Halfway to the advanced journey! Finish 25 reps of Diamond Push Up to proceed to the next journey

Pull

Pull Up

You will get a big back that people dream of! Finish 12 reps to proceed to the next journey.
Shoulder

90 Degree Pike Push Up

You can try placing your feet on a higher surface and do them. But if you are still struggling to do them just continue to do regular pike push up. Finish 20 reps of 90 Degree Pike Push Up to proceed to the next level
Legs

Switching Lunges

Do you hate training legs? But training legs help you to correct muscle imbalance between upper body and lower body. Finish 30 reps of Switching Lunges to proceed to next journey
Core

Bicycle Crunch

Bicycle Crunches are crucial for they strengthen many parts of your core, such as the rectus abdominis, which stabalizes the pelvis and keeps the low back from extending as the legs move. Finish 60 reps to proceed to the next journey.

Full Body

Negative Muscle Up

This exercise train your push and pull strength which is helpful for your workout. Finish 5 reps to proceed to the next journey.










Intermediate Journey #5

Requirment:Diamond Push Up x 25, Pull Up x 12, Pike Push Up x 20, Switching Lunges x 30, Negative Muscle Up x 5

Working Part Exercises
Push

Clapping Push Ups

Time to start training some push explosive strength! If you can do 25 diamond push up, you can do this easily. Finish 30 reps of them to proceed to the advanced journey.
Pull

Pull Up

Finish 20 reps of it and we can finally proceed to the advanced journey!
Shoulder

Elevated Pike Push Up

Place your leg on a surface that is almost 1 meter above the ground and start doing them. You can adjust the difficulty by changing the height of the surface. Finish 20 reps of it to proceed to the next level
Legs

Assisted Pistol Squat

Time to learn some cool skills on legs! Hold on a stuff with only one hand and start doing them. Finish 15 reps for each leg to proceed to the advanced journey!

Core

Assisted L Sit

This is also a cool skill to learn to impress your friend. You can use some resistance band or bending your legs to make it easier. Do it for 1 minute to proceed to the advanced journey
Full Body

Negative Muscle Up

Same Exercise but finish 10 reps to proceed to the next journey.

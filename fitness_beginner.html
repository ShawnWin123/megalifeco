Intro to

The Beginner Exercise

Welcome to the Beginner's Exercise section! This section does not require any physical build as this section is for absolute beginner. Our goal is to complete 15 push ups and 30 pull ups with your leg on ground. In order to start, please select a level.








Before We Start

Time Spent

This section will cost you only 5 month or less to complete it as it is the most unchallenging section. During workout, you need do the workout for sets and reps. We will recommend you to do the whole workout for 3 sets each and rest for 3 minutes between each workout.

Recovery Time

Arrangement of workout time is very important for building strength and muscle as the muscle needs enough rest to recover from heavy exercise. We recommend you to do 5 workout sessions per week, but not it in a row. Do treat your muscle well!

Diet Control

If you want to build muscle and burn calories, diet control is one of key to achieve your goal. Otherwise, all of your workout will be wasted since you are just increasing the calories in your body that is burned during the workout. Learn more about diet control here!








Your Journey















Beginner Journey #1

Requirment: None

Working Part Exercises
Push

Wall Push Ups

Wall Push Up is the simplest exercise to start training your push strength. Try to do 20 reps to proceed to the next journey
Pull

Chin Up (Leg On Ground)

This exercise target your biceps and back. Find a bar that is at your chest height and start doing them! Finish 10 reps to proceed to the next journey.
Legs

Basic Squats

Just do the basic squat like how you do infront of MRAD, but dont place your feet too close as it will lead to injury. Finish 10 reps to proceed to next journey.
Core

Plank

Just get on the floor and plank! Squeeze your abs as hard as you ca until you finish it. Do it at least 20 seconds to proceed to next journey.










Beginner Journey #2

Requirment: Wall Push Up x 20, Chin Up With Leg On Ground x 10, Basic Squad x 10, Plank 20 s

Working Part Exercises
Push

Incline Push Ups

Find a surface that is at your belly height. Try to touch your chest with the surface and make sure to do it slowly. Finish 10 reps to proceed to the next journey
Pull

Chin Up (Leg On Ground)

Same exercise, but find a bar that is at your belly height so it will be more challenging! Finish 10 reps to proceed to the next journey.
Legs

Basic Squads

Again, Basic Squats, don't rush rep. Finish 15 reps to proceed to next journey
Core

Plank

Quick tip: Don't look at your timer and focus on keep your body on a straight line. This time hold for 30s!










Beginner Journey #3

Requirment: Incline Push Up x 10, Chin Up With Leg On Ground x 10, Basic Squad x 15, Plank 30s

Working Part Exercises
Push

Incline Push Ups

This time you will decrease the height of the surface till the height of your leg. If it is too hard you can spread out your leg. Finish 15 reps to proceed to the next journey
Pull

Pull Up (Leg On Ground)

Pull up is a little bit harder than chin up and it will target your back more! Finish 10 reps to proceed to the next journey.
Legs

Basic Squads

Again, Basic Squats, don't rush rep. Finish 20 reps to proceed to next journey
Core

Plank

Quick tip: Practice and train more, don't give up! This time hold for 40s!










Beginner Journey #4

Requirment: Incline Push Up x 15, Pull Up With Leg On Ground x 10, Basic Squad x 20, Plank 40s

Working Part Exercises
Push

Knee Push Ups

Place your knee on the ground and go down slowly. Try to go as low as possible. Finish 15 reps if possible!
Pull

Pull Up (Leg On Ground)

Don't GIVE UP! Finish 20 reps to proceed to the next journey.
Legs

Basic Squats

Idk what to say, just finish 25 reps to proceed to next journey
Core

Plank

Fight with your brain! This time hold for 50s!










Beginner Journey #5

Requirment:Knee Push Up x 15, Pull Up With Leg On Ground x 15, Basic Squad x 25, Plank 50s

Working Part Exercises
Push

Regular Push Ups

Finally you can start doing regular push up! Keep working on it, good luck!
Pull

Pull Up (Leg On Ground)

Roading to negative pull up but you have to finish 30 reps of this exercise first!
Legs

Basic Squats

I know this is boring but this is the last section you will do this. Finish 30 reps! ez
Core
